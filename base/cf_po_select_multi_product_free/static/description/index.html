Purchase Order Select Multiple Product

in just a few clicks

This module provides features to Add Multiple Products in Purchase Order lines from Purchase Order.
Create Purchase Order of multi product selected from product list view.

-- "Select Products" button in Requests for Quotation form view. --

-- When you click "Select Products" button wizard will open. --

-- When you click "Add an item" another wizard will open and select products as you want and press select button. --

--- Now, selected products added in "Select products" wizard and now select Scheduled Date then press "apply" button. ---

--- Products are automatic added from wizard to order lines. ---

--- In Product tree view, Select products as you want then click on "Action --> Create Purchase Quotation" ---

--- In wizard, select Vendor and Schedule date and hit "Create Purchase Quotation" button. ---

--- Now, Purchase Quotation created and selected products added in order lines. ---
